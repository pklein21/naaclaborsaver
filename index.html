Methodology. Benchmark rates are regional estimates derived from a maintained state-level base-rate table (independent-shop national benchmark ≈ $140/hr), with multipliers for dealership (×1.25), diesel (×1.15), and EV (×1.20) service. Figures are fair-market reference points for adjusting and quoting — not live quotes from individual shops.   Live data. Facility listings, ratings & hours via Google Places (OpenStreetMap fallback), geocoding via Zippopotam, VIN decoding via NHTSA vPIC.   North American Auto Care · internal use.
