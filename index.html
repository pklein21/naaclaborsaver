Methodology

Benchmark rates are regional estimates from a maintained state-level base-rate table (independent-shop national benchmark ≈ $140/hr), with multipliers for dealership (×1.25), diesel (×1.15), and EV (×1.20). Reference figures for fair-rate adjusting — not live quotes from individual shops.

Data sources

Facility listings, ratings & hours via Google Places. Geocoding via Zippopotam. VIN decoding via NHTSA vPIC. OpenStreetMap used as fallback.
